Role and Responsibilities

The Board of Trustees plays a crucial role in overseeing the strategic direction and financial health of Fashion House. Their key responsibilities include:

  1. Setting the organization's strategic vision and mission.
  2. Ensuring financial stability and oversight, including approving budgets and financial reports.
  3. Hiring, overseeing, and evaluating the Executive Director/CEO.
  4. Ensuring legal and ethical integrity and adherence to governing documents.
  5. Representing the organization and advocating for its mission.
  6. Approving major policies and programs.

Board Meetings

The Board of Trustees meets annually to discuss organizational progress, make key decisions, and review performance. Meetings are closed to the public. For information regarding upcoming meetings or minutes from previous meetings, please contact us via our contact page.
